About Us

We are an engineering/distribution company that has been a supplier of various technologies to the Transcaucasian industrial market for many years and Owns a large portfolio of projects implemented in the field of automation. We serve more than 700 companies, including large key industrial companies. We have an exclusive representation of the world's leading factories, and based on many years of experience, we offer a wide selection of quality and reliable products. Engines, reducers, compressors, generators, equipment needed for control systems, high-tech sensors and other industrial equipment.
